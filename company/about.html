About Us

Building next-generation investigative platforms

Black Curate Labs is an intelligence technology company dedicated to building next-generation investigative platforms for modern law enforcement and financial crime investigations.

Our mission is to simplify complex investigations by transforming fragmented digital evidence into actionable intelligence through artificial intelligence, advanced analytics, and modern investigative technologies.

Our flagship platform, HashHound, has been conceived with a simple vision—to empower every investigator with a unified operational environment capable of supporting cryptocurrency investigations from initial intelligence development to operational decision support.

As digital assets continue to reshape financial crime, Black Curate Labs is committed to developing technologies that strengthen investigative capabilities, improve operational efficiency, and enable agencies to make informed, intelligence-driven decisions.

We are building technology not merely to analyse blockchain transactions, but to help investigators solve real-world cases with greater confidence, speed, and clarity.
